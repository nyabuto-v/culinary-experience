Simple Rice and Ndengu

Ingredients for cooking simple rice

  • Water
  • Rice
  • Salt to taste

Ingredients for Ndengu

  • 1 cup of cooked dengus /green grams
  • 1 Onion diced
  • 2 Tomatoes Dicedt
  • 2 cloves of Garlic diced
  • 1 Medium Green Pepper (hoho) diced
  • 1 carrot diced
  • ¼ tsp of pepper
  • 2 Royco or Maggi cubese
  • Oil to fry
  • Salt to taste

Procedure for cooking rice

  1. Pour water (of every cup of rice, use 1 and 3/4 cups of water) into a large saucepan with a tight-fitting lid. Bring to boil. Mix 1 teaspoon salt into water
  2. Pour in rice and add it to the boiling water.
  3. Stir once, or just enough to separate the rice. Use a wooden spoon to separate any clumps. Don’t over-stir: That can cause the rice to become sticky.
  4. Cover the pot and simmer. Be sure the lid fits tightly on the pot. Turn down the heat to its lowest setting. Let rice simmer for about 18 minutes, then remove from heat and allow the rice to steam in the pot for another 5 minutes.
  5. Just before serving, gently fluff the rice with a fork to separate the grains.

Procedure for cooking ndengu

  1. Fry onions and garlic for 1 minute
  2. Add the tomatoes, carrots, green pepper, salt, pepper, seasoning and cook until soft
  3. Next, add the cooked dengu and stir
  4. Next add in water the same level with the green grams. Cover and boil for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve your dengu recipe with rice
