Fried rice and Beans

Ingredients for fried rice

  • 2 Cups of Rice
  • 1 Onion Diced
  • 1 Green Capsicum (Green Bell Pepper) diced into small pieces
  • 1 Red bell capsicum (sweet pepper) cut in strips
  • 1 Carrot diced into small pieces
  • Oil to fry
  • Salt to taste

Ingredients for Beans

  • Perfectly boiled two cups ofkidney or red beans
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of royco
  • Two onions chopped
  • Two tomatoes chopped
  • One or two cloves of garlic, crushed and chopped
  • 1 teaspoon curry powde
  • Oil to fry

Procedure for cooking rice

  1. Wash riceand soak it in water. After 10 minutes drain the water and cook rice. Set aside.
  2. In a cooking pot, heat oil and fry onions. Add diced Green Capsicum (Green Bell Pepper), carrots and Red Bell Capsicum (sweet pepper) and cook for 7 minutes. Add the cooked rice, salt and stir to make sure the already fried ingredients coat the rice well
  3. Remove from fire and set aside
  4. Serve the dish and enjoy!!

Procedure for cooking beans

  1. In a cooking pan fry onions
  2. Add tomatoes and fry until tender. Use a cooking pot to press the tomatoes while cooking to make them soft quickly. Add garlic, Cardamom seeds, curry powder, Royco, cinnamon and salt and stir for 1 minute.
  3. Add beans and cook for 30 seconds
  4. Garnish with dry or fresh Parsley or Coriander and serve with fried rice
