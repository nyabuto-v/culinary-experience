  • Juice

Ingredients Needed

  1. A fruit of choice maybe a mango.
  2. A blender.
  3. A knife.
  4. A peeler.

Procedure.

  • Peel the mango.
  • Cut the mango flesh into little pieces.
  • Pour the pieces in a blender.
  • Blend the pieces of the fruit to a thick liquid.
  • Pour the juice into a glass.
  • Serve while cold.
