Ugali

Ingredients:

  1. 4 cups of water
  2. 2 cups of maize flour/cornmeal

Procedure:

  • Pour water to a heavy bottomed saucepan and bring to a boil
  • Add a handful of the maize flour/cornmeal to the boiling water.
  • Using a flat wooden spoon, stir the mixture to form a porridge-like consistency.
  • Continue adding the maize flour a little at a time while pressing to the sides of the saucepan to remove any lumps.
  • If there are no more lumps stop stirring and let it cook for about ten minutes.
  • At this point the mixture has become firm. Stir with the wooden spoon again and let it cook for a further three to five minutes.
  • Form the mixture into a round shape by bringing together the dough from the sides of the saucepan to the mixture. Transfer to a flat plate by turning the saucepan over on top of the plate.
  • If need be, the ugali can be cut into smaller chunks.

Sukumawiki

Ingredients:

  1. 1 large bunch Sukuma Wiki(cut into thin shreds)
  2. 1 large Onion(sliced)
  3. Garlic(optional)
  4. 1 Tomato(diced/sliced)
  5. 2-3 Royco cubes

Procedure

  • Make sure every ingredient is ready before you start cooking as this is a fast cooking procedure.
  • Cook the onion with little oil in a suitable pan/pot until soft or translucent then add the sukuma and tomatoes and stir. Do not allow to brown.
  • Cook while covered for about 2 minutes before stirring again.
  • Add 2 royco cubes at this point by crushing them with your fingers as well as the diced tomatoes and stir.
  • Allow to cook further for about 2 more minutes or until the sukuma wiki is cooked and bright green in colour.
  • Taste and add 1 more of the cube if necessary until you get your desired taste. DO NOT ADD ANY SALT as the cube has enough salt.
  • Serve hot or with your UGALI.
