Githeri.

  1. Pre-boiled maize and beans in equal amounts
  2. 4 Tbsp vegetable oil
  3. 1 onion, chopped (200g)
  4. 1 tomato, peeled and chopped (200g)
  5. 3-4 carrots chopped
  6. 3-4 potatoes cut into pieces
  7. 2 MAGGI Onion and spices tablets
  8. Salt

Procedure.

  • In a pot, fry the onions in the oil, add tomatoes and cook for a few minutes.
  • Add the carrots and potatoes and cook until almost done.
  • Add the boiled maize and beans.
  • Crumble in the MAGGI Onion and spices tablets.
  • Add a little water to make a sauce and cook for 5 minutes.
  • Serve hot, alone as a main dish or as a side to any other dish.
