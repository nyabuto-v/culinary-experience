recipe

sphagetti with beef stew

Ingredients:
  • tablespoons Vegetable oil
  • 1 Small onion chopped
  • 4 Large garlic cloves minced
  • 2 teaspoons Cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander seed
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne powder or to taste
  • ¾ pound ground or minced beef
  • 2 small red potatoes chopped small (optional)
  • 1 Carrot chopped
  • 1 Tomato chopped
  • 2 cups pasta sauce (prepared tomato sauce)
  • ½ package spaghetti (8 oz.) cooked and drained
  • 2 tablespoons Fresh cilantro chopped
  • 2 cups low-sodium chicken stock
DIRECTIONS
  • In a large pot, heat oil. Add onions and cook until tender, about 5 minutes.
  • Add garlic, cumin, coriander, and cayenne. Cook for 1 minute.
  • Add beef and cook until brown, 3 to 5 minutes.
  • Add stock, pasta sauce, potatoes, carrot, and tomato. Bring to a boil.
  • Reduce heat and simmer until vegetables are tender, about 20 minutes.,
  • Serve hot over warm pasta. Garnish with cilantro.
